The Victory

After placing 2nd in 2024, our CyberForce team came back with a vengeance this year. Taking first place at the 3rd Annual FCS Cybersecurity Competition was an incredible experience that taught us valuable lessons about preparation, teamwork, and performing under pressure.

What Made the Difference

~/blog/fcs-competition/strategy

Several key factors contributed to our success this year:

  • Role specialization: Each team member focused on specific domains - network security, forensics, web exploitation, and cryptography
  • Practice infrastructure: We built a lab environment that mirrored competition scenarios
  • Communication: Real-time coordination using shared documents and voice chat
  • Time management: Prioritizing high-value challenges and knowing when to move on
